In this specific homework problem, as were tasked on using the concept of thermal resistance networks to highlight the function of thermal barrier coatings of gas turbines. 

This specific homework problem was valuable in reinforcing the idea of thermal resistance networks, and how the idea of a driving force, flow, and resistance can be applied similarly to how circuit analysis is done to thermal systems. Not only was this good practice for learning about thermal resistance networks, but it also taught about a method of raising efficiency- adding thermal barrier coatings. When solving  this problem, it becomes apparent how adding TBC can help a system's efficiency and safety. Pre-TBC, the gas engine was unable to be maintained in accordance to the given specifications. Post-TBC, the engine's out temperature matched the needed specifications in order to be properly maintained!
